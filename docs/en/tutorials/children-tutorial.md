# Children's Tutorial — Build Your First Device Project

**Time:** 15 minutes  
**You need:** A grown-up for USB and the first firmware flash  
**You do not need:** To type commands

## Step 1 — Open Utah Flux Studio

Ask a grown-up to:

1. Run `Install Utah Flux.bat` once.
2. Double-click `Start Utah Flux Studio.bat`.

A colorful app opens in the browser.

## Step 2 — Try “Hello Screen”

1. On the left, under **Starters**, click **👋 Hello Screen**.
2. You will see two bricks on the board connected by a line.
3. Plug in the M5Stack with USB.
4. Click **🔌 Connect Device** and pick the device (Chrome or Edge).
5. Click **▶ Play Project**.

You should see **Hello!** on the screen.

## Step 3 — Build your own idea

1. Drag **When Device Tilts** onto the board.
2. Drag **Show Message** below it.
3. Click the **right white dot** on the tilt brick, then the **left white dot** on the message brick.
4. Click the message brick and change the text to your name.
5. Click **Play** and tilt the device!

## Step 4 — Add sound

1. Drag **Play Sound** onto the board.
2. Connect the tilt brick to the sound brick too (one trigger, two actions).
3. Pick **high** pitch in the settings on the right.
4. Play again — tilt and listen!

## Step 5 — Save your masterpiece

Click **💾 Save Project** and choose where to save.  
Next time, use **📂 Open Project** to load it.

## Brick cheat sheet

| Brick | What it does |
|-------|----------------|
| 🚀 When Project Starts | Runs when you press Play |
| 📐 When Device Tilts | When you move the device |
| 💬 Show Message | Words on screen |
| 🎵 Play Sound | Beeps |
| 🎉 Party Mode | Fun colors and sound |
| 🛡️ Safe Mode | Slows down to protect device |

## If something goes wrong

- **No connect button?** Use Chrome or Edge, not every browser works.
- **Nothing on screen?** Ask a grown-up to check USB and firmware.
- **Read the Live Log** on the right — green lines are good, red lines need help.

You built a real hardware project like Lego. Great job!
